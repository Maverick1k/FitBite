## About

FitBite is designed to help users take control of their health and well-being by providing personalized and comprehensive solutions for nutrition and exercise. FitBite is more than just an app; it's your partner in building sustainable, healthy habits, with an emphasis on user-friendly tracking and actionable insights.

> **Mission:**  
> Empower individuals to achieve a better lifestyle through balanced nutrition and effective workout routines, tailored to each user's unique needs and goals.

## Features

- **Food Search:**  
  Look up the nutritional value of foods and determine if they are healthy choices.

- **Diet Tracking:**  
  Log your meals throughout the day, track calorie intake, and get a summary of daily consumption.

- **Calorie Calculator:**  
  Calculate the total calories consumed from your meals and compare against your goals.

- **Exercise & Calories Burned:**  
  Select from a variety of exercises (running, walking, swimming, jumping, cycling), input your weight and workout duration, and calculate calories burned.

- **Workout Plans:**  
  Access guided workout routines for different muscle groups (Push, Pull, Legs) along with visual aids and instructions.

- **Healthy Recipes:**  
  Discover delicious and healthy recipes, available through subscription, to complement your nutrition plan.

- **Blogs:**  
  Explore blogs for tips, motivation, and knowledge sharing.

- **User Profiles:**  
  Personalized dashboards for each user to monitor progress and manage subscriptions.

- **Trainer Recommendations:**  
  Get links to online trainers for further guidance and learning.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- PHP (for backend scripts)
- Web server (e.g., Apache)
- Basic HTML/CSS/JS knowledge for customization

### Installation

1. **Clone the repository:**
   ```sh
   git clone https://github.com/Maverick1k/FitBite.git
   ```
2. **Set up your server and place the files in your web root directory.**

3. **Ensure PHP is configured and enabled on your server.**

4. **Open the application in your browser via `main.php` or `main.html`.**

### Directory Structure

- `main.php`, `main.html` - Home page/dashboard
- `features.php` - Core functionality (food search, calorie tracking, exercise calculator)
- `workout.php`, `pull.html`, `legs.html` - Workout guidance and routines
- `profile.php` - User profile management
- `blog.php` - Blogs section
- `recepies.php` - Recipes (subscription required)
- `about.php` - About FitBite
- `img/` - Images and icons
- `*.css` - Stylesheets
